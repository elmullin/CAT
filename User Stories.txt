As a player, I want each level to build on top the previous one, so that I can progress as a character and am continuously incentivized to finish the game/storyline.2. As a competitive gamer, I want my score to persist through levels, so that I can compare my final scores to other players of the game.3. As a social player, I want to be able to play my game with other people, so that I can game with friends and meet new people.
